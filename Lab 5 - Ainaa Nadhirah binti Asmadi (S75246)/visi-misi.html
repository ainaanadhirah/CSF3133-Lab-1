UMT Vision & Mission

UMT’s Philosophy

The integration of knowledge and practice based on faith in Allah is fundamental to the university’s endeavor in preparing competent human capital for global prosperity.

Vision & Mission

Category Description
Vision A marine-focused university, reputed nationally and respected globally.
Mission Generating knowledge for the prosperity of the community and global sustainability.

Educational Goals

Producing competent graduates who possess the value of ihsan for society’s well-being and environmental sustainability.

Slogan

Ocean of Discoveries, for Global Sustainability.

Niche Area

Marine Science and Aquatic Resources.

Functions

  • Support the university’s mission to become a premier centre of learning and research, contributing to the progress of humankind and knowledge creation for national development.
  • Provide trained manpower with professional skills, self-discipline, and positive work ethics.
  • Produce graduates sensitive to management ideas, responsive to change, and serve as role models for students and community.
  • Serve society by disseminating ideas, practices, and solutions to contemporary problems.
  • Establish relationships with other universities, institutions, and industries for mutual benefit and national development.
  • Support the university in research, learning, and technology exploration while providing quality services.

Objectives

  • Explore all areas related to Science, Technology, and Natural Resource Management through research.
  • Provide modern facilities (labs, libraries, computing, environment) to support knowledge development.
  • Offer excellent programs meeting current and future needs, producing responsible, knowledgeable, and competent graduates.
  • Actively contribute to social, economic, and educational development, focusing on local communities and Malaysians in general.
